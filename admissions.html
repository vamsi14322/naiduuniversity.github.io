Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our academic community. Here are the key steps to apply:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit your academic transcripts and standardized test scores.
  3. Write a personal statement outlining your goals and aspirations.
  4. Attend an admissions interview if required.

Application Deadlines

Stay informed about important deadlines for submitting your application:

  • Early Decision: November 1
  • Regular Decision: January 15
  • Graduate Programs: Varies by department
